# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and inclusive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any abusive or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments or contributions that violate this policy.
